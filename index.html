Qualitative & quantitative analysis of municipal water using IOT

Project

On daily basis we find many problems faced by people, when municipal water comes at irregular time, due to this water gets wasted. Our project mainly focuses on using IoT technology for switching on and off the pump automatically, water level indication, water quality analysis using sensors and building an application for the user which describes water level, water quality(like portable to drink or not), visual data on consumption of water on daily, monthly, yearly basis per house .Which indirectly helps in saving water.
